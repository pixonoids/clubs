Team Hermetica - Li-ion Battery

The lithium-ion battery is a rechargeable battery but overheating, short lifetime and under-performance in extreme temperature limit its comprehensive applications. To unravel this problem, Team Hermetica has come up with a project on the Thermal management system of battery. We have designed a jacket containing Nanofluid. This Nanofluid has a high cooling tendency, lowering the battery's temperature. Thus, heightens its operating range and efficiency.
