ENERGY THEFT DETECTOR

Power theft is the stealing of electricity through various means like meter tapping, physical obstruction or by direct hooking from line. This is quite difficult to detect, therefore this project introduces a ML based model that will predict future bills using previous records along with suspecting energy theft through constrained analysis.
