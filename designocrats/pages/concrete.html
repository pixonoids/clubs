Designocrats - Concrete Pipe Housing

India, the country of diversity, country of culture, and the country of people. India, is projected to have 16.8 percent of the world population by 2050 and reach rank one on the most populated countries. These figures are shocking, but whats more shocking is the area of land remains same to house these people. Which implies that architects must now come up with innovative, small, but effective housing schemes. Hence introducing concrete pipe housing, a well furnished, well equiped, small and effective way of housing. Concrete pipe housing accommodates your minimum needs to housing, by fitting everything in a small concrete pipe. Whats better is that this type of housing is aesthetically appealing, sufficient and budget and eco friendly.
