Automatic dish washer

In our country most of the foods are oily, hence a lot of effort is required for their washing. In hotels, restaurants, etc. there are a lot of dishes to be cleaned, which requires a lot of human effort and is very difficult. In our project, we are trying to improve the automatical cleaning ability by using scrubbers and well-designed and separated chambers for stepwise cleaning with minimum amount of water.
