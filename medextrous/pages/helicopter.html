Multi-use hexacopter

Multi – Use HexaCopter is an all purpose drone with 6 powerful motors that can lift weight up to 9 kg’s . These specifications make it suitable for use at emergency conditions like fire extinguishing, military operations inclusion.With the help of pick and place capabilities it can be used for parcel and medicine deliveries. Hexacopter offers more stability and can perform all these tasks with much of ease. These all applications give Hexacopter an upper hand from the normal drones.
