INDIRECT SOLAR DRYER

This project aims to provide an alternative way to the farmers for the purpose of drying their crops. In the conventional method used, there is a high loss of nutrients as well as high chances of degradation of the crop. It provides a better way of crop drying with minimum chances of any harm to the crops. We have been working on this project from last six months.

    OBJECTIVES :

  • To improve the quality of the crops.
  • To protect the crop from the germs, microbial attack, animals, and erratic weather conditions.
