Team Hermetica - Biofuel from Biowaste

The lack of food waste management and rapidly depleting fossil fuels is not an alien topic for anyone. Petroleum products are extremely polluting, and the electricity produced by them is prohibitively costly. Over the years, researchers have come up with a variety of solutions to these problems, but each has its own set of drawbacks. So, in order to address both the problems together, Team Hermetica has come up with a new technique, Biofuel from Bio-waste. This project aims at extracting the majority of energy from the waste food and turn it to fuel and electricity.
