Team OJAS

Team OJAS the technical club of Electrical engineering department spread the light of new ideas and enlightens about outstanding technologies available in the field of electricity. It is a part of the annual techfest NIMBUS. Events and exhibits are organized every year with the objective to provide a platform for the students to showcase their talent with a competitive spirit. It is not just about the competitions but also fun events where a lot of those new ideas are seen live.
