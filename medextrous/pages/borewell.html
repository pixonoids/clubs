Borewell child rescue system

Team MEDextrous presents you "Child Rescue System" in NIMBUS 2021, The Annual Tech Fest of NIT Hamirpur. This system is designed for the quick and safe rescue of children struck in borewell. This is more efficient then present technique use and can be really helpful for quick rescue operations. While not in use with other attachments this can be used as pipe cleaning and inspection system.
