Description:

Objective:

This project aims to create an illusion of telepresence with the help of an Arduino operated bot, a VR headset and some other components.

Abstract:

This robot with a camera is placed in a remote location to capture the environment in visual form. The captured visuals are displayed on the user’s virtual reality (VR) headset. An added feature allows the camera to move in the direction of the user’s head movements. This gives the user a real-time experience as if he is present where the virtual telepresence robot is located.

Execution:

• First, a 4 wheeled bot is made using Arduino Uno. It can be controlled through an app installed on the user’s smartphone.
• A camera is mounted on the bot which is capable of horizontal and vertical movement with the help of 2 servo motors.
• The camera’s video is viewed by the user on a smartphone in a VR headset. The headset is equipped with an MPU6050 sensor.
• The sensor keeps track of the user’s head movements and sends this data to Arduino Uno, with the help of servo motors, moves the camera accordingly, thus creating an illusion of telepresence.

Applications:

• Exploring new and unknown locations.
• To get a virtual experience of being present at a distant place (robot’s location).
• To spy on dangerous locations.
• Save human lives by alerting them about possible dangers.
• To prevent the spread of infectious diseases.
• For virtual tourism.
