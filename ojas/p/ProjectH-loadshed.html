ENERGY CONSUMPTION OF INDIVIDUAL DEVICE WITH TIME BASED LOAD SHEDDING

The common households and operating units rely on the cumulative electricity bill at the end of the month to account for their electricity usage. The device load monitor with programmable meter allows to keep a monetary check on the electricity usage. Time based load shedding allows a machine or a device to run for a stipulated period of time. In this system we can enter the start time as well as the duration.

    OBJECTIVES :

  • To calculate and display the electrical energy consumption of the load in terms of both units and money.
  • To use an individual device productively for a desired period of time.
