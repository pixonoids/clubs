Designocrats - Underwater city

The idea of humans living underwater may not be as crazy as you think. An idea once reserved for video games or science fiction, underwater cities may be a viable solution for humanity in the distant future. Would you pack up your bags, clear out your apartment, and move to an underwater paradise? Perhaps you might even dream of living like the fictional city of Atlantis. If it makes sense to go out and colonize Mars, the ocean is just as liveable and is far closer to home. As you probably already know, the earth is 71% water. This could be prime real estate for future generations.
