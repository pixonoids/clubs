Semi autonomous amphibious rover

This Nimbus, Team MEDextrous presents the "Semi-Autonomous Amphibious Rover". The Rover is a driver-less land/water transport inbuilt with an intelligent control system. The rover is enriched with Robotic Arm ,Sensors ,Cameras, Arduino, GPS. It is multipurpose, can be used for naval surveillance purposes, inspection deep tunnels, sewage monitoring etc.
