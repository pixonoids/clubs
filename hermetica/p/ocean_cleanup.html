Team Hermetica - Ocean Cleanup

"When the last tree has been cut down, the last fish caught, the last river poisoned, only then will we realise that one cannot eat money".
With this quote in mind, we devised a ground-breaking sailing technology known as " The Ocean Cleanup". A boat that moves like a snake and is operated remotely, travelling freely through the environment and cleaning the oceans. We're using cellulose sheets packed with polysulphide polymer, which is made up of refining and petroleum by products. The polymer is hydrophobic by nature. If the polymer has absorbed the oil, it will turn into a sponge, and the oil will be squeezed out using rollers, allowing the sheets to be reused.
