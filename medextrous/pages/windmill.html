Venturi windmill

Team MEDextrous presents you "Venturi Windmill" from NIMBUS 2021, Annual Tech Fest of NIT Hamirpur. Electricity scarcity is a big issue in many villages as well as in some cities. With the increase in various technologies (like electric vehicles and automation) we need to work on electricity production. The most economical way of producing electricity is the wind energy. Venturi windmill collects the air from all directions and transfers it to Venturi tube.
