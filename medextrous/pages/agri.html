Multipurpose solar agriculture equipment

Agriculture is the main occupation of a vast population in our country. Through our project, we aim to mechanize agriculture in order to improve productivity and provide an effective alternative to small scale farmers. A solar panel is used to charge the batteries. Different attachments will be connected at the front/rear end of the basic structure. These attachments are capable of performing different operations such as sowing, ploughing, spraying and cutting.
